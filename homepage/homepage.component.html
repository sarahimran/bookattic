How Does Book Attic Work?
Login/Signup Login to our website with your email and password. If this is the first time you are visiting, make sure you sign up to create a new account!

Buy a Book Browse through hundreds of books from a variety of genres. After placing an order for a book, it will be delivered to your door within 3 working days.

Sell a Book If you have a few books you wish to sell, you can easily create a listing for each of them, specifying price, condition, and other details.



Our Vision

Book Attic was created with a simple purpose: To bring together all book lovers under one roof. With the start of our venture, we hope to make book buying and selling across the city easier than ever, as we seek to encourage the love of reading in everyone.
